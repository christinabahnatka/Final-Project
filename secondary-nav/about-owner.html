About the Owner

Sharon Meeker

Trimmers owner Sharon Meeker has been a stylist for 30 years. Sharon strongly believes in the importance of continuing her education in the beauty industry and staying inspired with current trends. Always refining her craft, Ms. Meeker stays extremely dedicated to the relationships and needs of her clients. "I feel extremely privileged to go to work, meet amazing people, make them look and feel great, all while doing something that I love and am excited about." She is passionate about creating a comfortable environment, where her clients leave feeling confident, renewed, and beautiful from the inside out.
